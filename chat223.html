    About Me

    I'm Cyber Coder - NY.BMW.WT, a passionate full-stack developer based in Mutare, Zimbabwe. With a keen eye for detail and a love for clean, efficient code, I create digital experiences that not only look great but perform exceptionally well.

    I specialize in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to craft captivating digital experiences that blend creativity and performance. My expertise extends to building sleek, responsive websites and intuitive mobile apps using modern tools like Bootstrap, Flutter, and Firebase.
